AWARENESS PROGRAM ABOUT MENSTRUAL HYGIENE - 03.05.2023

The MIT Health Centre held a menstrual hygiene education workshop at Mega Mess on May 5, 2023. The chief guest was the medical officer Dr. T. Bharathi, who provided information on the value of menstrual hygiene for maintaining health and how to deal with uncomfortable menstruation symptoms. A total of 73 girls and women engaged, interacted with the doctor, and overcame their menstrual cycleobstacles to gain benefit.
